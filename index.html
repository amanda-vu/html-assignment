About Me

Hi! My name is Amanda Vu. I was born on April 1st and I grew up in Ottawa. I take the Interactive Media Design program at AC. I took this program because I enjoy working with design and taking this program seemed like a good chance to learn various skills for many different design career paths like a web developer, video editor, graphic designer, photographer, etc.

I've enjoyed drawing and art since I was a little kid. I liked it because it was a way to express creativity on paper and it was always satisfying to see the final product of great efforts. Drawing was also a stress reliever for me and it was something that was able to give me peace of mind. As I got older, I realized I really like designing things. I design things like clothing, shoes, posters and logos.
